Lore

The Imperial Imps are an army of 5000 Imps sent to the Human Realm to wreak havoc and bring forth misfortune. They originate from the Underworld where they are spawned, trained, and commanded by The Prince of Darkness himself. Imps are often described as troublesome and mischievous, widely seen as harmless tricksters. But the truth is Imps are a dark omen. Imps are the living embodiment of the dark times still to come. If an Imp is left unchecked for an extended period of time, a cloud of confusion, anxiety, and anger will set upon the human settlement they’ve been seeded in. This in turn causes turmoil in the settlement, causing chaos, riots, and political imbalance which leads to the eventual loss of trust in leadership, anarchy, and all-out civil wars.

It should now be abundantly clear why these Imperial Imps must be delt with promptly. But Imps cannot be killed as simply as one may imagine, they are immortal. If an Imp is impaled by blade, he simply turns to Ash and spawns elsewhere to continue his reign of terror. Therefore, the only way to put an end to the Imp infestation is to contain each individual Imp. Thankfully, our team has managed to find a way to do just that!

Solana Crystals are ancient artifacts blessed by the Almighty himself. They are made to attract, capture, and house the soul of evil entities such as the Imperial Imps. If a Solana Crystal is brought close enough to an Imp, it will create a vacuum to extract the Imp’s soul and trap it within the crystal’s inner chambers. As long as the Solana Crystal is kept safe, the Imp’s soul will be unable to escape, purging the Human Realm of these foul demons for good.
